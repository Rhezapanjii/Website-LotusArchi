When investing, trust and confidence on the investment products is a luxury to be proud of.

To answer the needs for safety and comfort in investing, we always provide the state-of-the-art security features that we gradually improve.

LA Track

LA Track is a one-stop solution for you, proud owners of Lotus Archi Gold. With the features specifically tailored to your needs LA Track is here to provide you with the safety and comfort you desire.

Our state-of-the-art security feature allows you to authenticate the Lotus Archi Gold in your hand, just as easy as pointing the camera and a few clicks away.

Here are the steps to check the authenticity of your Lotus Archi Gold :

  1. Open the app LA Track (or if you don’t have it yet, you can download it on Google PlayStore or Apple AppStore)
  2. Get in the menu “Scan QR Code”
  3. Point your camera to the QR Code on your Lotus Archi Gold
  4. See the result

For extra piece of mind, we also provided the newest feature in the application LA Track, which is Tracking. With this feature, you can now more than just whether it is an authentic Lotus Archi Gold your are holding, but you can also find out where the gold has previously been, as an extra layer of security.

Each and every one of our Lotus Archi Gold has a unique serial number, backed by a system from a renowned security company, guaranteeing your data integrity and privacy, so you can rest assured that each gold can be checked for its authenticity.

CertiEye

Also, collaborating with one of the producers of the safest packaging in the world, Lotus Archi Gold CertiCard is equipped with several security features at once. Here are some steps you need to do to ensure the authenticity of your Lotus Archi Gold CertiCard edition.

  1. Look for any sign of physical tampering. If our packaging has been tempered, the clear case in the middle will be fogged, and you won’t be able to see the gold it carries.
  2. Direct a UV light to the back of the packaging. An original Lotus Archi Gold that hasn’t been tampered will have writings “Seal Intact” visible on it.
  3. Download the application “CertiEye” on AppStore or Play Store, and scan the pattern on the bottom left corner of the packaging. If our logo show up, then you know it’s an original Lotus Archi Gold.
